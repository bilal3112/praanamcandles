The Praanam Philosophy

At Praanam, we believe in the power of scent to create moments of peace and elegance. Inspired by India's rich heritage of aromas, our candles are meticulously handcrafted using all-natural soy wax, premium fragrance oils, and cotton wicks. Each candle is a testament to our commitment to quality, sustainability, and the art of modern luxury.
