The Praanam Philosophy

At Praanam, we believe in the power of scent to create moments of peace and elegance. Inspired by India's rich heritage of aromas, our candles are meticulously handcrafted using all-natural soy wax, premium fragrance oils, and cotton wicks. Each candle is a testament to our commitment to quality, sustainability, and the art of modern luxury.

Our Process

Hand-Poured

Each candle is poured by hand in small batches to ensure the highest quality and attention to detail.

Cured to Perfection

We allow our candles to cure for two weeks, allowing the fragrance to fully bind with the wax for a long-lasting scent.

Ready for You

Once cured, each candle is carefully inspected, packaged, and sent to you, ready to light up your moments.
